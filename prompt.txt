Build a premium, high-converting Landing Page for a 'Side Hustle' offer (UK/AU market) using React, Tailwind CSS, and Lucide-React. 

### 1. Structural Requirements
- **Interactive Sticky Header:** Logo on left, a 'Desktop-only' nav in center. On Mobile, use a slide-out Hamburger Menu (Lucide 'Menu' icon) containing the Nav links and a 'Join Now' button.
- **Dynamic Top Bar:** Above the header, add a slim, 40px 'Earnings Ticker' with a horizontal scrolling animation (marquee) showing: "User @Liam04 just earned $15.60... User @Sarah_AU just earned $15.60..."
- **Comprehensive Footer:** 4-column layout. 
    1. Short 'About' snippet. 
    2. 'Quick Links' (Home, Tasks, FAQ). 
    3. 'Legal' (Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Cookie Policy, Impressum).
    4. 'Social/Trust' (Icons + 'Secure SSL' badge).

### 2. High-Conversion "Side Hustle" Adjustments
- **Scarcity Engine:** In the Hero and near the final CTA, add a 'Live Spot Tracker' component: "Hurry! Only [Dynamic Number] spots left in your area." (Animate the number ticking down from 7 to 2).
- **The 3-Step Income Flow:**
    1. **Step 1: Application** (Secure your slot).
    2. **Step 2: Device Verification** (Crucial: Mention this triggers the $15.60 payout).
    3. **Step 3: Payout** (Instant credit to PayPal/Amazon).
- **Regional Trust:** Automatically detect or simulate regional text for 'United Kingdom' or 'Australia' in the copy.

### 3. UI/UX & Motion Design
- **Animations:** Use Framer Motion (if available) or Tailwind 'animate-in' for fade-ups on sections. Implement a 'Pulse' effect on the primary CTA buttons.
- **Hover Effects:** - Buttons: Scale up (1.05) + brightness boost + shadow-xl on hover. 
    - Cards: Border-color shift from slate-200 to emerald-500.
- **Transitions:** Use 'transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out' on all interactive elements (links, buttons, inputs).
- **Visual Gaps:** Use `space-y-24` between major sections. Use `gap-8` for grid elements.

### 4. Direct Response Copy (PAS Framework)
- **Headline:** "Turn Your Phone Into a $15.60/Task Income Stream."
- **Subtext:** "Premium early-access for UK & AU testers. No experience needed. Verified daily payouts."
- **CTA Text:** "Check Availability & Start Now" (Add a small 'ArrowRight' icon that slides on hover).
